About

Established in 1998, West End is one of projects in MINDS MYG, the voluntary wing of the Movement for the Intellecutally Disabled of Singapore. We conduct weekly sessions every Saturday from 2.30 pm to 5.30 pm for a group of intellectually disabled trainees at Woodlands Gardens School with the aim of allowing them to pick up skills useful for independent living while having fun in a supportive and friendly environment.

Our Vision

West End Project will strive to become an established voluntary hub to support all MINDS centres by providing quality training and services to our trainees in the direction towards independent living and eventually open employment.

Our Mission

Channel our well-trained and independent trainees to open employment under the provision of our dedicated, motivated and well-trained volunteers.
